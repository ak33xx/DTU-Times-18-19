The Leadership Moment

"During one of the freshers, a girl tripped over the OAT stairs and fell. Nobody came to help her solely because she was differently-abled and I decided to step in. That moment gave me the motivation to take up the initiative to build ramps for the differently-abled students. Subsequently, this made me firm enough to join University politics to accomplish such tasks, where I learnt a lot from my seniors and contemporaries, and learnt the most from my juniors "

The Bicycle Buddies

We had a lot of fun at a cricket series in IIT Kanpur. The campus was huge and we faced a lot of inconvenience in walking around, especially in the night. For the hostellers, cycles were the main vehicle and a few even kept them unlocked always. We both wanted to go to a faraway canteen at 3 A.M. So we picked out 2 random cycles to ride - one was unlocked but the other was locked. Then, one fist punch, and it came loose! We continued going all around the campus on those cycles only for the rest of our stay! Of course, we returned them later! *Sheepishly smile*

One for the team

Pratibimb

We had an imposing play, 'Pehchan Kaun', which used to get great audience response but no wins. So we resolved to change this trend. We came up with a new concept, a new play, 'Meherbaani'. We had to perform in Thapar. I couldn’t join the team. After the results were out and I called, everybody was crying. I knew we had won. They cried there and I got emotional here in my room.

The Mysterious Case of Hampoo

Life lesson: Embarrassing stuff spreads like wildfire in College. My teddy bear's name was Hampoo, which was also part of my e-mail address. So someone secretly created a Facebook account for the soft toy. I still don't know who the creator is! -So... what is Hampoo up to nowadays? He's into a lot of social service. Inviting friend lists to Blood Donation camps, for example.

Placed

"I see myself in a cockpit of a Boeing or an Airbus flying at a height of thirty thousand feet burning jet fuel for a living." - Verdict? "Beginning work at their office this summer."
