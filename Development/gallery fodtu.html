The Fortunate of DTU

"Most of us live in a PG or hostel and roam around the campus till late in the evening. We have our own ‘adda’ near the TnP building. We love exploring the hotspots of our campus, although DTU Lake was a disappointment." "B.Tech students are usually awestruck on seeing the healthy gender ratio in our department. Their reactions are really funny to watch when they come to know about the number of girls in our class."

The Normies of DTU

“I had attended one of those unofficial freshers’ parties. The most interesting thing that happened during the party was the blind date. People were enjoying a lot, getting paired up with others but I couldn't even find an interesting partner!”

The Dog Lover of DTU!

“My favourite spot in the campus is the Mini OAT. There are puppies over there. Four, and I’ve named them all: Joey, Dude, Bro. Their mother is really scary! She barks at me whenever I touch them.”

The Worn Out of DTU!

"We tire out from playing in the sports complex and to add insult to the injury, we then have to walk a whole kilometer to reach the gate." “I would have ordered a pizza but I know that even after spending so much money on it, my roommate would be the one eating it.”
