BHANGRA - DTU

DTU Bhangra is a Dance Team whose members are committed to keeping traditional folk dance alive.

NRITYANGANA

Nrityangana is the official classical dance society of DTU. The members are motivated to keep the traditional artform of India alive.

KALAKRITI

Kalakriti is the official Creative Arts Society of Delhi Technological University.

MADHURIMA

Madhurima is the Music Society of Delhi Technological University. It includes members which either excel in western music, or classical music.

PANACHE

Panache, the Fashion and Designing Society DTU aims to provide an opportunity to it's members to develop their personality and enhance their confidence.

PARCHAYI

Parchhayi is the official photography and film-making society. It conducts relevant competitions in the same domain.

PRATIBIMB

Pratibimb is the Dramatics Society of DTU. The members are divided into 2 teams: Stage and Street. They participate in various dramatics competitions around the year.

SAHITYA

Sahitya is the Literary Society of DTU. It includes debating and conducting events like “Guftgu”.

VIBE

Vibe is the Western Dance Society of Delhi Technological University.
