SOCIETY OF ROBOTICS - DTU

SRDTU is a society for over-achieving robotics enthusiasts. They deal with projects which helps not just the society, not only the university but also the nation in very cost efficient way.

IEEE

IEEE DTU, the oldest student branch in Delhi Section of Asia Pacific region is devoted to its aim of instilling scientific temperament in students. The society is oriented towards both hardware and software fields.

INTERNATIONAL ORGANISATION OF SOFTWARE DEVELOPERS (IOSD)

IOSD DTU provides a platform for developers to come together and create projects, discuss ideas and learn in a competitive healthy environment. The society is oriented towards software development and coding.

SOCIETY OF CIVIL AND ENVIRONMENTAL ENGINEERS

Society for Civil and Environment Engineers(SCEE) is a society of DTU which works towards providing practical and innovative paths of learning.

CENTRE FOR ADVANCED STUDIES AND RESEARCH IN AUTOMOTIVE ENGINEERING

Centre for Advanced Studies and Research in Automotive Engineering was founded with the objectives of developing a variety of alternative fuels and engine technologies to reduce dependence on imported crude petroleum and reduce harmful exhaust emissions. The centre has seen a radical growth in last 17 years and it is considered as one of the key research centres not only in India but abroad also. The faculty along with students have published research papers in a large number of International Journals and have also attended international conferences.

AMERICAN SOCIETY OF MECHANICAL ENGINEERS

ASME DTU aims to promote increased collaboration amongst students and mutual development for all the members, thus achieving holistic growth through various experiences. The society is involved in the field of Mechanical Engineering.

SOCIETY OF PHYSICS AND ADVANCED RESEARCH IN ELECTRONICS

Team Okami Racing, the Electric Motorcycle Team of DTU, aims to design and manufacture high performance electric motorcycles to nurture the advent of full electric - emission free modes of transportation.

SOCIETY OF AUTOMOTIVE ENGINEERS

SAE(Society of Automotive Engineers) is a non profit engineering and scientific society dedicated to the advancement of mobility community.

MATHS AND COMPUTING SOCIETY

MACS - Mathematics And Computing Society of DTU is a breeding ground for the Mathematics and Computer Science enthusiasts.

INDIAN INSTITUTE OF CHEMICAL ENGINEERS (IICHE - DTU CHAPTER)

IICHE DTU's is to prepare students for professional practice, graduate study, and lifelong learning. They aim to promote and stimulate the advancement of chemistry and related sciences amongst the students by means of organized discussions, conferences, seminars, and workshops. They also encourage and support original chemical research, and application of chemistry.
